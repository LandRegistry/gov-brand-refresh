Header and account links

This design is for services that have user accounts. Your service name goes in the navigation panel and if your service has a landing/home page then you can use the service name as a link to this. Account navigation for signing in/out and going to user accounts goes in the navigation bar, aligned right.

The GOV.UK Design System contains full advice on using the service navigation component.

Use the 'Sign in' and 'Sign out' links to navigate and view the full design.

  • If the logo should be a link to the HM Land Registry landing page.
  • Design for hover, focus, active and visited states of the logo if it is a link.
  • The colour of the footer background and top border.
